# Jumbler Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 15, 2026  
**Last updated:** July 15, 2026

> Draft for publication. Replace every bracketed placeholder before publishing.

This Privacy Policy explains how **Univation Games** ("Jumbler," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the Jumbler mobile game and related services (the "Service").

For privacy questions or data requests, contact **[privacy or support email]**. Our public privacy-policy URL is **[public privacy-policy URL]**.

## Information We Collect

### Account and authentication information

When you create or sign in to an account, we may process:

- Email address and authentication identifiers.
- Username and profile information you choose to provide.
- Account plan, role, and sign-in or password-reset activity.

Passwords are handled by our authentication provider and are not stored by Jumbler in readable form.

If you use Google sign-in, Google may provide account identifiers and basic account information according to your Google account settings and Google's privacy policy.

### Gameplay and social information

We process information needed to operate the game, including:

- Game mode, difficulty, board and scramble state, word submissions, paths, scores, and game timestamps.
- Daily challenge progress, hidden-word status, clues, tokens, relics, achievements, streaks, and reward claims.
- Leaderboard, friend, room, trade, and multiplayer information when those features are used.
- Reports, support requests, and other information you send to us.

### Device, app, and notification information

If you enable notifications, we may process an Expo push token, notification preference, time zone, platform, app build, and a limited device label such as device brand and model. We use this information to deliver notifications, synchronize local-time challenges, prevent duplicate device registrations, and troubleshoot compatibility.

The app may store session, onboarding, preferences, and temporary gameplay state locally on your device.

### Camera and QR scanning

Jumbler requests camera access only when you choose to scan a friend's QR code. Jumbler does not record or upload camera video or images. The QR value detected by the scanner is sent to the Service to process the friend connection.

### Advertising

Jumbler may show rewarded advertisements through Google Mobile Ads. Google and its advertising partners may process information such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from IP, ad impressions, ad interactions, diagnostics, and fraud-prevention signals, subject to their policies and the choices available on your device and in your region. Rewarded ads are used to unlock eligible in-game rewards, such as clues.

### Purchases

If premium purchases are enabled, purchases are processed by the relevant app store and RevenueCat. Jumbler receives purchase and entitlement information needed to provide premium access. Jumbler does not receive or store your full payment-card number.

### Service analytics and security logs

We may record gameplay events, mode and difficulty, feature usage, error information, and security or anti-cheating signals. We use these records to operate the Service, improve balance and reliability, investigate abuse, and protect the game.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

1. Create and secure accounts and authenticate users.
2. Provide gameplay, daily challenges, clues, rewards, relics, leaderboards, social features, and purchases.
3. Deliver requested notifications and maintain local-time scheduling.
4. Validate scores, detect cheating, prevent duplicate rewards, and enforce game rules.
5. Diagnose crashes, latency, failed requests, and compatibility issues.
6. Measure and improve the Service, including game balance and feature performance.
7. Respond to support requests and communicate important service or policy changes.
8. Comply with law and protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the Service.

## How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share or make information available to:

- **Service providers**, such as Supabase for authentication, database, and server functions; Google for sign-in and advertising; Expo or related push-notification infrastructure; RevenueCat for purchase entitlements; and app stores for billing and distribution.
- **Other players**, when necessary for features you choose to use, such as usernames, leaderboard entries, friend connections, rooms, or trades.
- **Authorities or other parties**, when required by law or reasonably necessary to prevent fraud, abuse, security incidents, or harm.
- **A successor organization**, if Jumbler is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets.

We require service providers to process information for authorized purposes and to apply appropriate safeguards, but their own privacy policies may also apply.

## Data Retention and Deletion

We retain account and gameplay information while it is needed to provide the Service, maintain records, enforce rewards and game integrity, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, or protect the Service. Retention periods may differ by data type.

To request account deletion or deletion of personal information, contact **[privacy or support email]** from the email address associated with your account and include your Jumbler username. We may retain limited information when necessary for fraud prevention, legal compliance, security, or legitimate recordkeeping. Aggregated or de-identified information may be retained.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including authenticated access controls and encrypted connections where supported by our providers. No service can guarantee absolute security, so please use a unique password and protect your device and account credentials.

## Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or receive a copy of your personal information, and to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. You may also control camera, notification, advertising, and purchase permissions through device or app-store settings.

To exercise a privacy right, contact **[privacy or support email]**. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request. You may also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority.

## Children

Jumbler is not directed to children under 13, or the higher minimum age required in the user's jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below that age. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact **[privacy or support email]** so we can investigate and delete it when appropriate.

## International Processing

Jumbler and its service providers may process information in countries other than the country where you live. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

## Third-Party Services

The Service may link to or use third-party services, including Google, Google Mobile Ads, Supabase, Expo, RevenueCat, Apple, and Google Play. Their handling of information is governed by their own privacy policies and terms. We encourage you to review those policies.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when the Service, our data practices, or legal requirements change. We will update the effective date and provide additional notice when required. Your continued use of the Service after an update means the revised policy applies to future use.
